Topics that must be covered

    What will be covered in this journey? (Mathematical foundations for AI)

    Linear Algebra: Vectors, matrices, tensor operations, eigenvalues, eigenvectors, SVD, norms

    Calculus: Derivatives, partial derivatives, gradients, chain rule, integrals, optimization

    Probability: Bayes' Theorem, conditional probability, distributions (Gaussian, Bernoulli, Poisson, Beta, Dirichlet)

    Statistics: Mean, median, mode, variance, standard deviation, hypothesis testing, p-values, confidence intervals

    Optimization: Convex functions, global vs. local minima, gradient descent intuition

    Information Theory: Entropy, cross-entropy, KL Divergence, mutual information

    Graph Theory: Basics for graph-based models (GNNs, knowledge graphs)

    Numerical Methods: Stability, convergence, and iterative solvers
